About Tango FC

Tango Football Club is more than just a team – we're a family united by passion for the beautiful game. Founded in 2015, we've grown from humble beginnings to become Masvingo's most successful football organization.

Our commitment to excellence, both on and off the pitch, has earned us five league titles and countless memorable moments. We believe in developing not just skilled players, but well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to our community.
